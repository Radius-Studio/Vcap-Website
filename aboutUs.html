Video Collaboration and Production (VCAP)

Drexel VCAP offers a variety of services designed to provide our clients with the latest video and collaboration technology support. We make presentations viewable to more people, lectures available to more students, and information instantly accessible to a Web-wide audience. The VCAP team is your main point of contact for support with Zoom for meetings, events and courses that require video conferencing for remote participation. We provide video production, live-streaming, and podcasting services. VCAP is also an excellent resource for A/V Consultation, classroom installs and A/V technology purchases. We have extensive knowledge in classroom technologies, collaboration spaces, and the project management process.

Audio Production and Podcasting

The Video Collaboration and Production Team operates an audio production suite in the lower level of The Korman Center. Our team can assist with the creation of high quality podcasts and lecture captures. The Video studio also comes complete with a 4K production camera and a teleprompter for smaller in scope video productions.

Get in touch with us today to book space and time with an engineer!

Check out one of the podcasts we produce, Slushpile!

Video Production

VCAP offers production services to faculty and staff within the Drexel community for use within your online courses, departmental websites, or marketing campaigns. We have professional and prosumer production cameras, lighting packages, a camera-mounted teleprompter, audio recording equipment and much more. With some advanced planning we can even incorporate basic motion graphics, title slides and even music.

If you are interested in producing a high-quality video please contact us to make arrangements.

For a sample of some of our studio-quality productions, please see this showcase of our videos on our Youtube account.

Zoom Videoconferencing

Access Your Zoom Account via SSO on the Zoom App with your Drexel Credentials or on your browser at drexel.zoom.us
Ensure that the company domain is correct, drexel.zoom.us. Click Continue.
SSO will bring you to this menu. Select SIGN IN STANDARD. (If you are conducting business with medical patient information, select SIGN IN HIPAA.)
Sign in using your regular Drexel Credentials and click CONNECT.
Can't Login? On the Zoom Login Page, do NOT type in your email and password directly. Use SSO (Single Sign On) which will allow you to sign in using your Drexel Credentials.
**If you are experiencing issues with Zoom please submit a ticket to: ZoomAdmin@drexel.edu.**
